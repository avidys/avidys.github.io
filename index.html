Biometrics Services

  • Study Coordination /Project Management

    • Acting as project coordinator or manager for your study, we define timelines with the stakeholders and insure that each task is achieved on time, escalating as necessary. This service is offered either as a complementary service or as a standalone service.

  • Case Report Form Design and Development

    • We have created several Case Report Forms in specific therapeutic areas. From the visits and items specified in the protocol we can generate specific forms according to your design specific needs. At your convenience, we can propose standard forms from our Form Database to minimize costs. Input elements for this phase are the protocol and its annexes as well as your internal CRF standards or CDISC/CDASH recommendations The output is a set of PDF documents ready to be printed as well as versioning and modification history.

  • Data Control Definition and Formal Testing

    • Data Controls or Edit Checks are the core of the Clinical Data Management work. We offer services to help define, develop and implement standards control or study specific controls. Unit testing up to complete system validation are part of the services we offer.

  • System Validation

    • We offer services to help comply with the industry requirements regarding validation. Having a number of years of experience in testing and validating systems, we propose the related services: validation plan, URS, FS, matrices, design specifications, testing, validation report, and audit.

  • Database Audits

    • During the study progression, or at the end of the study, we can perform database audits based on several criteria. Risk scenario and acceptance criteria are discussed with you before the audit takes place. A formal report will be provided and any deviation to predefined standards or requirements.

  • SOP development

    • We don't supply pre-written SOPs (we would call them recommendations or requirements). We believe that the technological diversity of data works implies that technical standard procedures have to be customized according to local values, practices, and available tools. This SOP write-up can be performed on site, or we can meet your key personnel for project review and validation.

  • Site Audits

    • We provide audits in specialized technical areas (Data Management systems, Validated Systems, QA systems, Sponsor or site organization). We also provide technical site audits focused on the security requirements for open systems, database audits and QA systems audits centered on the technical aspects.

  • CDISC/SDTM package preparation
  • SCDM certification preparation
